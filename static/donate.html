Make a Donation. Support the site and help to keep it going!

This site is developed and supported by one person working on his spare time. It was born out of a personal need to get my twitter feeds via RSS after twitter shutdown their RSS service.

Since then I have found that there are a lot of people who appreciate such a service.

I really hate to have to ask for support and donations from the site users. But the site itself generates zero funds, and costs me money to pay for hosting services.

So, if you are feeling generous, or just want to help support the site, you can show your support by sending me an Amazon gift card.

Gift cards can be purchased for as little as USD 0.15 and any amount will be appreciated. Please remember to send the card to rss4twitter.feedback@gmail.com

Donors get early access to new features and a dedicated URL with much less traffic and faster updates.

Again, thanks for all the support and the great feedback that I am getting from all of you. Your comments help this site become better and more useful.
